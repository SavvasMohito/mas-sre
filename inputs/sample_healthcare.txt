Telemedicine Platform Requirements

We want to develop a telemedicine platform connecting patients with healthcare providers for virtual consultations.

Core Requirements:

1. Patient Portal
   - Patient registration with personal and medical information
   - Secure login and multi-factor authentication
   - View and update medical history
   - Book appointments with doctors
   - Access prescription history
   - Upload medical documents and lab results

2. Provider Portal
   - Provider registration and credential verification
   - Manage availability and appointments
   - Access patient medical records
   - Prescribe medications electronically
   - Document consultation notes
   - Billing and insurance claims

3. Video Consultation
   - Real-time video calls between patient and provider
   - Screen sharing for reviewing documents
   - Chat functionality during calls
   - Recording consultations (with consent)

4. Prescription Management
   - Electronic prescription generation
   - Integration with pharmacy systems
   - Prescription refill requests

5. Payment & Billing
   - Accept payments via credit card and insurance
   - Generate invoices
   - Insurance claim submission

6. AI-Powered Features
   - Symptom checker chatbot
   - Automated appointment scheduling assistant
   - Medical record summarization using LLM
   - Drug interaction checker

7. Data Requirements
   - Store patient health records (diagnoses, treatments, medications)
   - Store consultation recordings and transcripts
   - Maintain audit logs of all data access
   - Support data export for patients (right to data portability)

The platform will operate in the United States and must handle sensitive health information. We expect integration with multiple Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems.
